Lore takes place over three days (12 turns) in a point-salad fantasy adventure. Just about everything gives you points: quests, treasures, relics, bits of history, defeated monsters, and even your character's fame.

Every turn in Lore goes through three phases, let us take a quick look at how a turn would go...

Event phase:
If the time of day matches your field's aura, you may draw an Event card and resolve it. If not, you skip this phase.

Movement phase:
You may move your character token to any adjacent field.

Action phase:
There are a total of six different actions available in lore and you may choose any one of the 4 Action cards underneath the Merchant board to perform during your turn.

After all players have taken a turn, advance the time of day.
